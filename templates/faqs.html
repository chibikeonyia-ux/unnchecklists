Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for common UNN registration concerns, plus department-specific guidance below.

Common UNN registration questions

What documents should I bring?

  • Admission letter printout from the UNN portal or JAMB.
  • O'Level result(s) and any supporting certificates.
  • Birth certificate or declaration of age.
  • Local government identification or attestation letter.

When do I pay the acceptance fee?

Acceptance fee windows change each session. Pay only within the official portal dates.

  • Confirm the deadline on the UNN portal.
  • Print your receipt and keep it for clearance.

What happens at faculty clearance?

  • Officials verify your originals against uploads.
  • You may complete departmental forms or interviews.
  • Bring all receipts and signed documents.

Can I register courses before clearance?

Most faculties require clearance first. Follow your departmental timeline.

  • Wait for clearance approval on the portal.
  • Use the checklist here to track each step.
