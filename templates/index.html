Step-by-step UNN registration guide

Use this guide as your quick reference, then log in to save progress on your checklist.

1

Documents to prepare

  • Admission letter printout from the UNN portal or JAMB.
  • O'Level result(s) and supporting certificates.
  • Birth certificate or declaration of age.
  • Local government identification or attestation.
  • Passport photographs and payment receipts.
2

Registration steps

  1. Accept admission and pay the acceptance fee on the portal.
  2. Complete your biodata form and upload required documents.
  3. Attend faculty and departmental clearance with originals.
  4. Register courses and print your final slips.
3

Deadlines to watch

  • Acceptance fee window (check the portal for exact dates).
  • Faculty clearance period and final signature dates.
  • Course registration close date after clearance.
  • Hostel or ID card windows if applicable.
